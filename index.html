Crabs

Spicy Crab

Spicy crab is a culinary delight featuring succulent crab meat infused with a fiery blend of spices. The exact composition varies widely depending on regional preferences and individual recipes, but common ingredients include chili peppers (providing the heat), garlic, ginger, scallions, and often soy sauce, rice vinegar, or other savory components for depth of flavor. The level of spiciness can range from a gentle warmth to an intense, mouth-numbing burn, catering to diverse palates.

Butter Garlic Crab

Butter garlic crab is a decadent and flavorful dish that features succulent crab meat bathed in a rich, creamy sauce made with butter, garlic, and often other aromatics like white wine, lemon juice, and herbs. The combination of buttery richness, garlicky pungency, and the sweet, delicate taste of crab creates a truly irresistible culinary experience.

Steamed Crab

Steamed crab is a simple yet elegant dish that showcases the natural sweetness and delicate flavor of the crab meat. It involves steaming whole crabs until they are cooked through, resulting in tender, juicy meat that is easily extracted from the shell. This method preserves the crab's natural flavors and is often served with a simple dipping sauce, like melted butter or a soy sauce-based mixture.

Fried Crab

Fried crab is a crispy and flavorful dish where crab meat or whole crabs are coated in a batter or breading and deep-fried until golden brown and crispy. The frying process adds a satisfying crunch and enhances the crab's natural flavor. Fried crab can be served as a standalone appetizer or as part of a larger meal. No matter which preparation you choose, crab offers a delicious and nutritious meal. Remember to enjoy it in moderation, as the sauces and cooking methods can affect the overall fat and calorie content.

Steamed Clams

Steamed clams are a simple yet flavorful dish that showcases the natural sweetness and brininess of fresh clams. They're prepared by steaming the clams in a pot with a small amount of water, often with aromatics like garlic, white wine, or herbs. The steam cooks the clams quickly, resulting in tender, juicy meat that is easily extracted from the shells. The broth that's created during the steaming process is also delicious, making it a perfect base for a flavorful soup or sauce.

Crustaceans

Grilled Lobster

Grilled lobster is a luxurious and flavorful dish that showcases the sweet, succulent meat of the lobster. The lobster is split in half, brushed with butter or oil, and grilled over an open flame until the meat is cooked through and the shell is charred and smoky. The high heat of the grill caramelizes the natural sugars in the lobster meat, creating a rich, complex flavor that is enhanced by the smokiness of the grill. Grilled lobster is often served with melted butter, lemon wedges, and fresh herbs, allowing the natural sweetness of the lobster to shine.

Steamed Lobster

Steamed lobster is a luxurious seafood dish where a whole lobster is gently cooked in a steamer, preserving its natural sweetness and delicate texture. The lobster retains its vibrant reddish-brown shell, while the flesh turns a delicate white or pink. Steamed lobster offers significant health benefits, being nutrient-rich with protein, vitamin B12, and omega-3 fatty acids, while remaining low in calories and fat. Its versatility allows for simple preparations with melted butter or more elaborate dishes with sauces and sides, making it a delicious and healthy choice for a special meal.

Fried Shrimp Tempura

Fried shrimp tempura is a Japanese dish. It features shrimp coated in a light batter. The batter is typically made from flour, cornstarch, and water. The shrimp is then deep-fried until golden brown and crispy. The resulting texture is a crispy exterior and a juicy interior. The shrimp has a mild, slightly sweet flavor. Shrimp is a good source of protein. It also contains omega-3 fatty acids, beneficial for heart health. Shrimp is rich in selenium, a powerful antioxidant. While deep-frying adds fat, tempura shrimp can be relatively low in calories if the batter is light. Enjoy it as part of a balanced diet.
