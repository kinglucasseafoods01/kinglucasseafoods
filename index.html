Lapu-Lapu Fish

The Lapu Lapu fish, also known as Grouper, is a popular fish in Southeast Asian cuisine. It's a white, firm-fleshed fish with a mild flavor. It's often served steamed, grilled, or fried.

Crabs

Crabs are fascinating crustaceans with ten legs and a hard shell. They scuttle sideways and use their large claws for defense and catching food. They come in many varieties, living in oceans, freshwater, and even on land. Cooked crab, on the other hand, is a delicious seafood delicacy! The meat has a sweet and delicate flavor and a tender, flaky texture. It's often enjoyed in dishes like crab cakes, crab bisque, or simply steamed with butter.

Prawns

Prawns are delicious crustaceans with long, slender bodies and delicate, sweet flavor. They're often called "shrimp" but are actually a distinct variety. They are enjoyed worldwide, cooked in various ways, from grilling and frying to steaming and adding to soups and stir-fries.

Shrimps

Shrimps are small, tasty crustaceans with a delicate, sweet flavor. They have a curved body and are often found in oceans and freshwater. They're a popular seafood choice worldwide, enjoyed in various dishes like stir-fries, pasta, and soups.
